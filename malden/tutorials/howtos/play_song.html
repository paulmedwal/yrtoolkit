1. Add a Button

In User Interface, click and drag a Button into the Viewer.

2. Add a Player

Now click Media, and drag a Player into the Viewer. It will show up under the non-visible componenets.

4. Change Player source

In the Player properties, click on the source property, select song.mp3 or upload your sound file, and click ok.

5. Add when Button1.Click

Click Blocks.

In Blocks, click on Button1 and drag out the when Button1.Click block.

6. Call Player

Click Player1 add add call Player1.Start to when Button1.Click.
