1. Add a Label, a TextBox, and a Button

In User Interface, click and drag a Label, a TextBox, and a Button into the Viewer.

2. Add when Button1.Click

Click Blocks.

In Blocks, click on Button1 and drag out the when Button1.Click block.

3. Add set Label1.Text to

Click Label1 and drag out set Label1.Text to and connect it to when Button1.Click.

4. Add join

Click Text and drag out join and connect it to set Label1.Text to.

5. Add " "

Click Text again and drag out " " and connect it to the first socket of the join block. Change the text to say something else, such as "Hi there,".

6. Add TextBox1.Text

Click TextBox1 and drag out TextBox1.Text and connect it to the second socket of join.

Test out your app! When you type your name into the text box, does it reset the label to say "Hi there, (your name)"?
