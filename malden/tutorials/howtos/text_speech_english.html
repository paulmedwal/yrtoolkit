1. Add TextToSpeech

In Designer, click on Sensors and drag and drop TextToSpeech onto the Viewer. It will show up in the non-visible componenets section.

2. Add call TextToSpeech1.Speak

Click Blocks.

Click on TextToSpeech1 and add call TextToSpeech1.Speak into the when YandexTranslate1.GotTranslation block.

3. Add get translation

Hover over the red translate button again and click on get translation. Drag it to the "message" socket of call TextToSpeech1.Speak.

4. Test it out!

Press the Translate button and watch your speech translate and be spoken by your device!
