1. Connect with your device over wifi

Connect using the companion app so you can see your app on your mobile device.

Click here if you don't have a device and here if you don't have wifi or data available.

2. Download and install the MIT AI2 Companion App on your phone

Download from the Play Store by scanning the QR code below or by opening this link on your device.

If you can't use the Play Store click here.

3. Go through the installation instructions on your phone

After downloading, step though the the instructions to install the Companion app on your device.You need to install the MIT AI2 Companion only once, and then leave it on your phone or tablet for whenever you use App Inventor.

4. Open your app's QR code

Click Connect, then AI Companion.

5. Connect your app with your phone

A QR code similar to this should pop up on your screen.

Either scan the QR code or enter the code in the text box.
