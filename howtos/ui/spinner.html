Spinner Component

Components you need: Spinner (User Interface), Label (User Interface)

In Designer, click on Spinner1 go to the Properties panel. Add a list of words (I used cat, dog, bird) to ElementsFromString

Go to blocks and click on Spinner1.

Drag out the "when Spinner1.AfterSelecting" block.

Next, click on Label1.

Connect the "set Label1.Text" block.

Click on the red selection button.

Connect the "get selection" block.

You can add any other action and even have different events occur depending on the selection, like below. I used the "=" block (in Logic), the "get selection" block (hover over the red selection button), two labels (in Text), and the "set Label1.Text to" block (in Label1).
