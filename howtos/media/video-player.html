Video Player Component

Components you need: Video Player (Media)

In Designer, click on VideoPlayer1 and go to the Properties panel. Upload a movie from your computer in the Source section.

If you have a movie in the format .mov, convert it to .mp4 online.

In order to make your video play again once it's completed, in Blocks, click VideoPlayer1.

Add the "when VideoPlayer1.Completed" block and connect the "call VideoPlayer1.Start" block.
