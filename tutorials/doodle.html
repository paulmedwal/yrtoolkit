Digital Doodle

1. Introduction

This tutorial will show you how to draw a line on the screen as the user drags a finger around.

Connect using the companion app so you can test this on your mobile device. If you don't know how to connect, click here.

2. Add a Canvas

Click on the Drawing and Animation drawer and drag a Canvas component to your viewer.

3. Set Height and Width to "Fill parent"

In the Properties panel, click Height and set it to "Fill parent". Do the same with the Width.

4. Add when Canvas1.Dragged

Click Blocks.

Click Canvas1 and click and drag when Canvas1.Dragged to the Viewer.

5. Add call Canvas1.DrawLine

Click on Canvas1 again. Click and drag the call Canvas1.DrawLine block and add it to when Canvas1.Dragged.

6. Add get prevX

Go to the when Canvas1.Dragged block. Hover over "prevX" and click on get prevX. Add it to the "x1" socket of the call Canvas1.DrawLine block.

7. Add get prevY, get currentX, and get currentY

Next, hover over "prevY" and click on get prevY. Add it to the "y1" socket of the call Canvas1.DrawLine block. Repeat with get currentX and get currentX.

8. Test and extend your App!

Test your app out! Next, try to extend your app using these suggestions or come up with your own!

  • Change the color of the ink (and let the user pick from a selection of colors). You can check out the Snapchat Remix tutorial.
  • Change the background to a photograph or picture.
  • Let the user draw dots as well as lines (hint: Use DrawCircle block).
  • Add a button that turns on the camera and lets the user take a picture and then doodle on it.
