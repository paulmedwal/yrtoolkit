Encrypt Procedure

Components you need: Button (User Interface), Label (User Interface), TextBox (User Interface)

In the Blocks section of Screen1, click Button1.

Drag out the "when Button1.Click" block.

Click Label5.

Connect the "set Label5.Text to" block.

Click Procedures.

Connect the "call encrypt" block.

Click Math.

Drag out the "0" block and connect it to the shift socket. Change the 0 to a 3, or any other number you'd like to use for the shift.

Click TextBox1.

Connect the "TextBox1.Text" block to the text socket.

Try out your encryption on your device. You can change your shift to get different results.
