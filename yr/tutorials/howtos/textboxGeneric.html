Add a TextBox

Click and drag a TextBox to your Viewer.

Users can type into this TextBox.

TextBox Properties

TextBoxes have two properties that accept text: Hint and Text.

The Text property sounds just like what it is: You can enter text into the TextBox and it will show up in the box, just like a Label.

The Hint does NOT show up in the Viewer, but in the Companion or in the compiled app, will show up as lightened text in the background of the TextBox. Any text added to the TextBox will wipe out the hint text.

Getting TextBox Text

TextBoxes allow users to type in their own text. User text will appear as TextBox.Text.
