In Designer, click on Sensors and drag and drop TextToSpeech onto the Viewer. It will show up in the non-visible componenets section.

Go to the Properties panel and find country. We'll make our TextToSpeech component speak in Spanish. Go to the "Country" dropdown and choose "MCO" for Mexico.

Then go to the "Language" dropdown and choose "es" for Spanish.

Click Blocks.

Click on TextToSpeech1 and add call TextToSpeech1.Speak into the when YandexTranslate1.GotTranslation block.

Hover over the red translate button again and click on get translation. Drag it to the "message" socket of call TextToSpeech1.Speak.

Finally, go to when translateButton.Click and click on the "ru-en" block and change the text to "ru-es" so the text the translator translates to is Spanish.

Test out your app! When the button is pressed, it should translate from Russian to Spanish and speak the Spanish.
