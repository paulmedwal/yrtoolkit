Click on Sensors. Click and drag an AccelerometerSensor to the Viewer.

2. Add when AccelerometerSensor1.Shaking

Click Blocks.

Click on AccelerometerSensor1 and click and add when AccelerometerSensor1.Shaking to the Viewer.
